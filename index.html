About

The game was released on April 27, 2021 by DaeriSoft. It was developed by two high school students, Kwon Oh-jun and Heo Jae-ho.


The Spike: Cross - is a two-dimensional sports game that combines casual game elements with a charming anime art-style.

It offers a unique and enjoyable experience for volleyball enthusiasts and casual gamers alike. In the game, players take on the role of a young volleyball player,

who aspires to become a champion. The narrative gameplay traces the path of the protagonist from a beginner to an experienced athlete,

showcasing various challenges, training sessions and exciting volleyball matches. The game successfully captures the essence of the sport, providing an exciting and engaging volleyball experience.
